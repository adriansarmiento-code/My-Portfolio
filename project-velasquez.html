Project Overview

Velasquez Funeral & Chapel is a long-standing family funeral business in Cabiao, Nueva Ecija. The goal was to build a professional, compassionate digital presence that helps grieving families find and understand the services available to them â€” without the overwhelm.

The Challenge

The business had no online presence. Families were relying entirely on referrals and phone calls, making it difficult to communicate pricing, packages, and available caskets. The owner needed a site that was easy to manage without technical skills, while projecting trust and professionalism.

The Solution

A full-stack Vue.js application was built with a clean public-facing website and a powerful admin panel. The admin panel allows the owner to update packages, caskets, blogs, and contact inquiries â€” all without touching code. TinyMCE powers blog creation, while Cloudinary handles all image uploads.

Overview

Velasquez Funeral & Chapel is a long-standing family business with no prior online presence. Families relied entirely on referrals and phone calls. The goal: a professional, compassionate digital home — and a CMS the owner can manage independently.

The Problem

Funeral websites require extreme sensitivity in tone and design. Beyond aesthetics, the admin needed to update packages, casket listings, and blog posts without touching code — requiring a robust, intuitive CMS layer.

My Role

End-to-end development — Vue.js frontend, Node/Express API, MongoDB data models, Cloudinary image pipeline, TinyMCE blog editor, and full deployment to Hostinger (frontend) and Render (backend).

Key Features

01
Service Packages

Three tiers — Basic, Semi-Elegant, Elegant — with detailed casket options and transparent pricing.

02
Casket Gallery

Browse wood, metal, and imported caskets with images, specs, and payment options.

03
Admin CMS Panel

Full content management for packages, caskets, and blogs — TinyMCE rich-text editing and Cloudinary uploads.

04
Blog / Resources

Grief support articles and funeral planning guides — fully managed via the admin panel.

05
SEO Optimization

Vue Meta for per-page SEO, Google Analytics, and structured semantic content.

06
Contact + Maps

24/7 contact info, Google Maps integration, and an inquiry form with admin-side message management.

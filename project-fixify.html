Project Overview

Fixify was built to solve a real problem: customers in Angeles City struggling to find trusted, verified professionals for home services. The platform creates a centralized location to browse, book, and review service providers across three core categories.

The Problem

Local homeowners often rely on word-of-mouth or untrusted online listings when searching for plumbers, electricians, or cleaning services. This results in wasted time, scams, and inconsistent service quality. Fixify addresses this by building a structured, review-driven marketplace with verified provider onboarding.

Solution & Approach

The platform was designed around three user roles: customers, service providers, and admins ‚Äî each with their own dashboard and authorization level. JWT authentication with role-based authorization ensures each user only accesses what they're permitted to.

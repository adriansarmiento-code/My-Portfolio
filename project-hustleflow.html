Overview

The Hustle Flow is a personal blog and content platform built and fully managed on WordPress. Beyond the technical setup, this project demonstrates content strategy, CMS management, SEO implementation, and an understanding of what drives readership and engagement online.

The Problem

Great developers understand the full picture — not just the code, but how their work drives results. Managing a blog end-to-end built practical skills in SEO, plugin selection, page speed, and content planning that directly complement full-stack engineering.

My Role

Full site configuration — theme installation, custom CSS overrides, Yoast SEO setup, Google Analytics integration, and ongoing content creation covering productivity, mindset, and digital skills.
